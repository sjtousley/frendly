Why we started Frendly

We are a team of former remote workers, entrepreneurs, and consultants who have all felt the pain loneliness working remote.

Yes, coworking spaces existed, but as entrepreneurs with little cash to spare, we couldn't stomach the $400 per month bill.

We decided to start Frendly as a free community to see if other remote workers want the social interaction without the expensive monthly coworking space bill.

Hopefully, we will grow a large enough network to turn this into a viable business (membership will always be free!) with the goal of bringing us all together.
